Admission Process

  1. Fill the online application form on the official MHT-CET portal.
  2. Appear for MHT-CET entrance exam (or JEE Main).
  3. Wait for the CAP (Centralized Admission Process) round results.
  4. Visit the college for document verification with original certificates.
  5. Pay the admission fee and confirm your seat.
  6. Collect your college ID card and fee receipt.
  7. Attend the orientation program before classes begin.
